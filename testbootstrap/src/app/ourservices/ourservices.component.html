  • General Tutoring

    Texas Tutors offers one-on-one tutoring for students K-12. We are able to offer long term and personalized attention to help ensure that students can succeed in school. Our tutors are held to a professional standard of lesson prep, academic knowledge, and work ethic so that our students can succeed. Although minors cannot be tutored on UT campus, our tutors are more than willing to meet at local libraries, coffeeshops, or school campuses (with proper permission). Though are services are free, we pride ourselves on helping students succeed.
